About

I'm a front-end web developer from Indianapolis, IN. I graduated from Purdue University in 2012 with a degree in acoustical engineering. After working in the field for about a year, I realized it wasn't really what I wanted to be doing, so I started learning web development, which I loved pretty immediately. To me, web development is the perfect mix between left and right brain. It's both visual and logical. I'm especially interested in responsive layouts. While I didn't love the work, I feel that engineering school did give me a great mind for problem solving that has translated very well to development.

I like to stay busy in my free time. When I'm not working, you can find me playing in a band, sleeping in the woods, exploring dungeons, and slaying dragons. A lot of the time, any of those activities will also be accompanied by a good craft beer.
